• Custom Pipes

    • Basic Custom Pipes

      • John is from London and he is working in Google Company

        Trimmer the string by using custom pipe :

        {{ "John is from London and he is working in Google Company." | basicPipe : "1" }}

        Adding some content about the "John" by using custom pipe :

        {{ "John is from London and he is working in Google Company." | basicPipe : "2" }}

        Adding some More content about the "John" by using custom pipe :

        {{ "John is from London and he is working in Google Company." | basicPipeWithMultiParams : "3" : "He is having the More than 5 Years in IT Industry . He is Married having 2 kids" }}
